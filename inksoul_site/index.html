The Official Inksoul Webpage

Welcome to the official Inksoul Webpage! For new visitors and ongoing fans alike, Inksoul is an upcoming comic series releasing in 2024 on the famous manhwa reading app, Webtoon. It takes place on the fantastical continent of Sylva; a world where anyone, even regular people, can develop strange powers known as "gifts". The story follows the protagonists Amy, Teddy, and Jack as they use their powers to combat a dangerous new threat to the world: entities known as "Inksouls". How will they accomplish this? How do these "gifts" even work? Explore the site to learn about the world, characters, and more!
